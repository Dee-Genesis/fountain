About Us

Dedicated to Excellence
in Professional Education

Fountain Professional Training Services is a professional training centre dedicated to excellence in Accounting, Business, Management Education and Professionalism. Based in Ibadan, Nigeria, we serve professionals across West Africa and beyond.

Our high quality of teaching fosters independent thinking and adaptability, enhanced by talks, workshops, and practitioner-led programmes that place your studies within a broader professional context.

🎯 Our Mission

Empowering professionals with globally recognised qualifications that advance careers and organisations.

🔭 Our Vision

To be West Africa's leading centre for professional consulting, education, and training.

⚖️ Core Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, and a deep commitment to professional growth.
