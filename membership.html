What is Professional Membership?

Membership of a chartered professional body is a globally recognised mark of competence, integrity, and professional standing. Members earn designatory letters (e.g. ACIML, MCILG, FCIET) to use after their name, signalling to employers, clients, and peers that they meet the highest standards in their field.

Post-nominal designatory letters

CPD programmes & resources
Digital membership certificates
