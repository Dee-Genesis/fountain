About Fountain Professional Training Services

01 What is Fountain Professional Training Services all about?
Fountain Professional Training Services is a management and educational consulting firm dedicated to delivering professional training, capacity development, and strategic consulting services. We support individuals, organizations, educational institutions, and public sector agencies in building competence, improving performance, and achieving sustainable growth.
02 Is Fountain Professional Training Services registered?
Yes. Fountain Professional Training Services is duly registered in Nigeria and operates in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements. This ensures that our services are delivered within a recognized legal and professional framework, giving our clients confidence and assurance in all engagements.
📋

Services & Programmes

03 What services do you offer?
We provide a broad range of services, including:
  • Professional training and development programs
  • Management consulting services
  • Educational consulting and institutional support
  • Corporate training solutions
  • Capacity building programs
  • Research, policy, and advisory services
  • Coaching and mentorship
  • Workshops, seminars, and conferences
04 Do you design customized training programs?
Yes. All our programs can be tailored to meet the specific needs of individuals, organizations, and institutions. We conduct a proper needs assessment before designing any training to ensure relevance and maximum impact.
05 Who can participate in your programs?
Our programs are open to a wide range of participants, including:
  • Corporate professionals and executives
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners
  • Public sector employees
  • Educational institutions and staff
  • NGOs and development professionals
  • Graduates and career seekers
06 Can organizations request customized corporate training?
Yes. We specialize in designing tailored corporate training solutions that align with organizational goals, industry requirements, and workforce development needs. Contact us to discuss your organization's specific requirements.
07 Are your certificates recognized?
Our certificates are issued upon successful completion of training programs. In cases where programs are delivered in partnership with professional bodies or aligned with industry standards, participants may also benefit from additional professional recognition or CPD accreditation — giving you credentials respected by employers and institutions across Nigeria and worldwide.
🎓

Training Delivery

08 Do you offer online training?
Yes. We provide flexible training delivery options to ensure accessibility regardless of your location:
  • Physical (onsite) training
  • Virtual / online live sessions
  • Hybrid learning models
09 How do you measure training effectiveness?
We evaluate our programs using structured methods to ensure measurable results:
  • Pre- and post-training assessments
  • Participant feedback and engagement analysis
  • Workplace performance improvement tracking
  • Organizational impact evaluation
10 Do you provide support after training?
Yes. Our focus is on ensuring long-term impact and sustainability. Post-training support includes:
  • Coaching and mentorship
  • Implementation guidance
  • Follow-up advisory sessions
  • Refresher training (where applicable)

Partnerships

14 Do you partner with professional bodies?
Yes. We collaborate with recognized professional bodies and industry associations — including AIET, CIBIA, CIET, CILG, CIML, and CIML Business School — to enhance the quality, credibility, and relevance of our programs. These partnerships help ensure alignment with global standards and may provide participants with professional certification pathways and CPD opportunities where applicable.
15 How can organizations partner with Fountain PTS?
Organizations can partner with us by reaching out for consultation. We develop partnership arrangements for training delivery, institutional development, and professional capacity-building initiatives. Contact us to begin the conversation — we are always open to building meaningful collaborations.
